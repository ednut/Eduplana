Eduplan is a civic development organization focused on developing the next generation on soft skills, non-cognitive skills and technical skills. We believe quality and inclusive education is a basic right of every child.

REDUCING OUT-OF-SCHOOL CHILDREN IN NIGERIA

According to the 2015 UNESCO institute for statistics, Nigeria has the highest number of out-of-school children with 8.7 million accounting for 47% of the world’s out-of-school children. The impact of this population segment on the nation’s economy has been draining and we can work together in reducing poverty while they also contribute positively to national development. In 2015, Nigeria lost over 1.88 trillion Naira and that figure could increase above 3 trillion Naira by 2030 if the present number of out-of-school children is not reduced.
